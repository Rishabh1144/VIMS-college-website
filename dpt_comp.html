Minimum qualification for admission

Candidates seeking admission to the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program should have successfully completed the 10+2 examination conducted by the respective state or central government authorities, recognized as equivalent to 10+2 examination by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). They must have studied English as one of the subjects, and should have passed with Mathematics as a compulsory subject. Additionally, they may have studied subjects such as Computer Science, Information Practices, or Business Mathematics as optional subjects individually. Any other qualification approved by the appropriate educational authorities in India, deemed equivalent to the above examinations, will also be considered.
