Table Tennis:

Table tennis is one of the most engaging and fast-paced sports offered at our college. Our infrastructure and support system ensure that students get the best platform to pursue this sport.

Indoor Facilities: Our college boasts a well-equipped indoor sports arena with professional-level table tennis tables and equipment.

Individual and Team Competitions: We organize regular intra-college and intercollegiate table tennis tournaments, allowing students to showcase their talents.

Expert Guidance: Dedicated coaches provide personalized training to enhance agility, reflexes, and technique.

Sports Scholarships: For students excelling in table tennis, we offer scholarships and avenues to participate in regional and national championships.

Kabaddi:

Kabaddi, an indigenous Indian sport, holds a special place in our college sports culture. We offer one of the best platforms for students to excel in this energetic and strategic game.

Specialized Kabaddi Court: Our college has a designated kabaddi court that meets official standards, making it perfect for professional practice.

Coaching and Training Camps: Under the guidance of experienced kabaddi players and coaches, students receive rigorous training in tactics, strength, and agility.

Tournaments and Recognition: Our college regularly hosts kabaddi tournaments and participates in national competitions, giving students ample exposure and opportunities to gain recognition.

Support for Sports Careers: Many of our kabaddi players have successfully moved on to professional leagues, and we offer all the support needed to make this transition smoother.

Badminton:

Badminton is one of the most popular and widely-played sports in our college. We are proud to offer excellent facilities and training to students who wish to pursue badminton competitively or recreationally.

Dedicated Badminton Courts: Our college has top-class indoor badminton courts equipped with professional-quality lighting and flooring to ensure the best playing experience.

High-Level Coaching: Experienced coaches provide students with regular training sessions to improve their skills, strategy, and fitness levels.

College Representation: Our badminton teams have a strong record in intercollegiate tournaments, and we encourage students to represent the college at state and national levels.

Pathway to Professional Sports: With regular exposure to competitions and guided support, students have a clear path to pursuing professional careers in badminton.
