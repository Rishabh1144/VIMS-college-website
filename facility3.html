Seminar Hall

The Seminar and Conference Halls act as platforms for students, faculty and corporate personalities for regular interfaces, conferences and other events. They are ready with all the electronic equipment and training aids essential for carrying out high profile academic and business events.

This dynamic space accommodates a variety of events, including guest lectures, industry symposiums, and student-led workshops. The layout is designed to promote engagement and collaboration, with comfortable seating and adaptable room configurations to suit different event formats.

The seminar hall plays a crucial role in enhancing the academic experience by providing a platform for knowledge exchange and networking opportunities. It serves as a venue for faculty discussions, research presentations, and career development sessions, fostering intellectual growth and professional development among students and faculty alike.

Overall, the seminar hall facility at our college is integral to promoting academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement.
