Health & Wellness

Our college offers comprehensive health and wellness facilities to support the well-being of our students. The on-campus health center is staffed with qualified medical professionals who provide a range of services, from routine check-ups to urgent care. This ensures that students have access to immediate medical attention when needed.

In addition to medical services, our wellness facility includes a modern gym equipped with the latest fitness equipment. Students can participate in various fitness classes such as yoga, pilates, and aerobics, promoting physical health and stress relief.

Mental health support is also a key component of our wellness program. The college provides access to licensed counselors who offer confidential therapy sessions and mental health workshops. These resources help students manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns effectively.

Moreover, our campus hosts regular health and wellness events, including health fairs, nutrition workshops, and mindfulness seminars. These initiatives aim to educate students on maintaining a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Overall, the health and wellness facilities at our college ensure students have the support they need to thrive both physically and mentally.
