Our Group

With the aim to introduce and promote higher education in Vindhya region of Madhya Pradesh DPR charitable trust came in existence in 2001.In 2002 it came up with the first private engineering institute of region in a small district Satna. In order to meet the rising aspirations of the youths various new courses got added gradually. Presently 5 Institutions of higher education and a CBSE affiliated school are successfully serving the society under the umbrella of DPR charitable Trust

Till now more than 18000 students have graduated from the intuitions. In the journey it has produced several state toppers. Its distinguished alumni are placed across the globe in various multinational corporates and are also serving in government organizations at various levels.
